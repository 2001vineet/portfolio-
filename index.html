A little about me

Notebooks before Figma.

I still start every project with a paper notebook — messy diagrams, arrows connecting half-formed thoughts, sticky notes everywhere. That habit shaped how I design: loose and exploratory early, precise and systemized by the time it ships.

Previously at a fintech startup and a healthtech studio, I've led research-to-prototype for real-world products.

My toolkit spans discovery interviews, journey mapping, rapid wireframing and hi-fi prototyping — always anchored in a sketch first, because thinking on paper is still the fastest way to find the right idea.
